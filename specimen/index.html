About me

I'm a web developer in New York City. I primarily work in JavaScript, and PHP; sometimes I dive into Python, Bash, and Lua. I'm a Vim and GNU/Linux user and enthusiast.

Currently, I work for Elite Daily as their Senior Frontend Developer. I've held similar roles at Time Inc., Arnold NYC, and Arnold Worldwide in the past years.
I was an Adjunct Instructor at Boston University CDIA for a while, where I taught web development and WordPress stuff.
I used to freelance regularly, but now I prefer tinkering with open source.
